How to Buy

Step One - Discover your item.

Buy, locate and discover by looking through the categories to find exactly what you’re looking for. You can also use the search bar to narrow down what you need.

Step Two - Read the Description.

Once you’ve located your desired item, make sure to check out the description in the About section to see how suitable it is for you.

Step Three - Make your offer.

Ready to purchase? You have to make a bid first and let seller know that you’re interested. Press the 'Place Bid' button and a pop-up shall appear. Type in the amount you would like to bid and click ‘Place My Offer’.

Step Four - Confirm your offer.

Congratulations! You placed an offer! If you made a mistake or want to cancel it for whatever reason, don't worry, you can. If the seller accepts your offer, you can meetup to pick up your item and pay. Check out the 'Payments & Getting Paid' tutorial for more information!
