stylefnf Refunds + Returns

Whether you can get a refund/return or not is completely dependent on the seller. They must have a return policy stated for their listing. This is an external issue and needs to be resolved through contacting the seller. It is recommended that you connect with the seller using our messaging service and reach an agreement.

Our company does not carry any responsibilities regarding refund/return policies.

Return Policy: There are three possible return policies the seller has to choose from.

1. No Returns

As the name implies, once the person has paid (either online or during meetup), there is no turning back.

2. Conditional Return

For buyers: if the item has some major flaws undescribed in the description, the buyer can return the product within 3 days of purchase and receive full refund as enforced by our employees. Examples of the situation described above: a math textbook with many pages torn, a sofa with a very large coffee stain, or a cell phone that randomly shuts down.

For sellers: if the buyer returns the item in a damaged condition, you have the right to not accept the return. This will be enforced by our employees as well.

3. Return Within 24 Hours or 3 Days

If the item is in the same condition as shown in the product images, the buyer can return and receive full refund of the purchased item within the given amount of time after purchase.

In case of a dispute regarding returns/refunds, please consult one of our employees. In the worst case scenario, we will ban sellers who will not comply with their chosen return policies and we will ban buyers who try to misuse the return policy system.
