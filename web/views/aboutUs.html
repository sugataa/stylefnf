About Stylefnf


Stylefnf is a startup out of the University of Toronto's Hatchery program (a startup incubator). Its founder, Sugata Acharjya, initially started the project at a Hackathon, originally under the name 'Haberdashery.' When he got a lot of positive feedback about his idea he decided to pursue it in order to revolutionize the way students buy and sell things on campus. There is no other system that caters to this need, which is surprising when you consider how many students there are that run into difficulties when obtaining furniture. One of the most important things to us at Stylefnf is trust; this is evident in the fact that everyone on the site requires a university email address to sign up!
