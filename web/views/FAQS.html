Sellers

1. How do I delete my listings?

First go to the listing you wish to delete. Right next to ‘Update My Listing’ there is a down arrow which will lead you to the option ‘Remove this Listing’. Once a listing is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

2. Why has my listing been removed?

There are two reasons for this: A) Your listing has been sold. Once your item has been successfully bought, it is moved to the 'Sold' section; check here first. B) It violates the conditions of Stylefnf or is inappropriate to even be listed. Contact our support for more details.

3. Is it free to list?

Absolutely! Listing on Stylefnf is free. Therefore, your first or 110th post will be of no charge. Once your item sells, however, you will be charged a small percentage (see payments guide) of the final price.

4. Why do I have more than one offer on my item?

Since we are a bid-based platform, some items will gain more interest than others. In this case, sellers have absolute authority to pick which offer suits them the best.

Buyers

1. Why do we place bids?

Stylefnf is a bid-based platform. Sellers, in a sense, auction out their items and choose the preferred bid. When that happens, you’ll be placed on hold.

2. What does being "on HOLD" mean?

This means that the item is technically yours but will be labelled as HOLD until payment is received. This is also to ensure that other buyers will still have a chance to purchase the item in case the original buyer is not able to.

3. Can I cancel my offer?

Yes! You are able to cancel the offer by going to the original listing page and clicking the red button that is labelled 'Cancel Offer.'

Payments

1. When will I be charged?

A small service fee will be automatically charged during the actual payment process; you’ll be charged the final total of the product when the seller has confirmed your bid and you are ready to pay.

2. Will I get a purchase receipt/invoice?

You will receive a receipt through the email address you have provided during your sign up. It will be sent to you automatically as soon as you complete your transaction.

3. What are the service charges? Are they the same for both sellers and buyers?

The service charges are different for both parties. For the seller, there is a service fee of 3% and for the buyer, there is a service fee of 6%.

4. Why are there service charges?

Service charges to the buyer are used to provide a better and more secure platform for our users to buy and sell; all the money goes into development and upgrades for improved features. Service charges to the seller are for covering the cost of credit card processing.

Refunds/Returns

1. Should I put a return policy for my item?

Stating your return policy is important. Let buyers know your policies on refunding and returning to help create a smoother transaction. If sellers state that they are open to returns/refunds, they enter the agreement that they are aware of issuing a refund for returned items and carry the responsibilities of fulfilling that agreement.

2. I'm having issues with returning an item, what should I do?

It is essential to have an agreement between both seller and buyer when returning an item. Mutual resolutions are encouraged by Stylefnf but if things are unable to work out (e.g. unable to get in touch with seller/buyer), contact our support system to open up a case.
