How does it work?

Here's how easy it is to sell


1. List an item

Add some pictures and plenty of details and you're set!

2. Accept an offer

Review your offers and potential payout, then hit accept.

3. Meetup

Chat with the buyer and pick a mutually convenient time and place to meet up.

4. Get Paid

Once the buyer hits Pay, you'll get a notification and the funds will be deposited in your account.
