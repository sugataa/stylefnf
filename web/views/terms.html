Common questions

I made a purchase recently and haven’t received it. Is it still on its way?

All orders will be shipped by August 7th, 2015, and can be tracked using the tracking number included in your shipping confirmation email. Note that all orders placed since July 20th, 2015 are final sale, and no refunds will be offered.

I sent a return recently. Will it still be processed?

Returns must be received by August 7th in order to be processed for a refund. Our warehouse will be closing on that date, so unfortunately any returns received later will not be processed. Note that all orders placed since July 20th, 2015 are final sale, and no refunds will be offered.

I have a check from a previous sell order that has not been deposited yet. Can I still deposit it?

Valid checks issued from Twice for sell order payments can be deposited until September 11th, 2015.

I haven’t responded to my sell order. What happens to it?

Any sell orders that haven’t been responded to will automatically be donated to Goodwill.

I still have Twice store credit. What happens to it?

If you have Twice store credit that you earned from selling your clothing to Twice, from a purchase refund where your original payment method was PayPal or credit card, or from a gift card that you or a friend purchased, you’ll be able to redeem your store credit for the equivalent cash value. Any store credit that you have from marketing promotions, referrals or any other source will not be redeemable.

Redemption requests must be received by August 21st, 2015, and all redemptions will be issued by paper check. Checks must be cashed by September 11th, 2015. Later redemption requests will not be honored.

Note: We are no longer accepting redemption requests for Twice store credit.

I still have questions. Can I email or call you?

We no longer offer support via phone or social media. We will be responding to urgent email support inquiries through August 7th, 2015.
